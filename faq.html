What documents are required for KYC verification?

To complete your verification at Bet Ninja Casino, you'll typically need to provide:

  • A valid government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or official government correspondence)

The KYC verification process at Bet Ninja Casino typically takes between 24 to 72 hours once you've submitted all required documents. During peak periods, processing may take slightly longer.
