Understanding Wagering Requirements

Wagering requirements are an essential aspect of online casino bonuses that every player should understand before claiming any promotional offer at Bet Ninja Casino.

What Are Wagering Requirements?

Wagering requirements, also called playthrough or rollover requirements, specify how many times you must wager bonus funds before you can withdraw any associated winnings. For example, if you receive a $100 bonus with a 35x wagering requirement, you must place $3,500 in qualifying bets before you can cash out.

At Bet Ninja Casino, wagering requirements typically range from 30x to 40x for deposit bonuses and 40x to 50x for no-deposit bonuses or free spins winnings. These requirements are clearly stated in the terms and conditions of each promotion.

Different games contribute varying percentages toward meeting wagering requirements. At Bet Ninja Casino, slots usually contribute 100%, while table games like blackjack and roulette may contribute between 10% and 20%. Live casino games often have reduced contribution rates as well. Always check the specific terms for each bonus to understand which games help you clear requirements most efficiently.
